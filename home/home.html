Why us

As long time cat owners, we appreciate that cats enjoy both attentive care, and a peaceful place to laze. So you can be sure your cat gets a top quality stay while your away.

WHAT DO WE PROVIDE

The K Street Cats is a relaxing country stay for your cat. Located on a small farm in beautiful rural Greytown, our cat accommodation is a renovated original farm barn providing cats with a rustic, homely environment quite different from staying in a vet clinic or pet shop style cat cage.

Attention

Cats are fed twice daily, either with your preferred feed (provided by you), or we can provided appropriate wet and dry food. Additionally cats are checked regularly through the day and there is an on-line camera in the barn for additional security.

Facilities

Cats stay in large indoor airy runs providing room to move around, climb and snuggle in cosy cat beds and cat climber frames. There is also the option of use of a large ‘cat lounge’ enclosure in the barn porch with open air and views to the farm.
