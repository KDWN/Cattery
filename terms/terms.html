TERMS AND CONDITIONS

1. Introduction

These terms and conditions provide a basis for acceptance of cats into the cattery and cover the whole period of the accommodation at the cattery. By applying for a booking at the cattery, customers are presumed to have agreed to these terms and conditions.

2. Costs

  • Charges are on a per day basis. Payment may be made in cash or via internet banking. We do NOT have EFTPOS or credit card facilities.
  • Cats dropped off after 4.00pm do not incur a fee for the day of drop off, similarly cats picked up before 9.30am on the day of departure do not incur a fee for that day. If the cat stays for a longer portion of either day, daily charge fees may be incurred.
  • Minimum charge per stay is equivalent to 2 days fee.
  • There is no discount for providing food.
  • Outstanding fees will be forwarded to a dept collection agency and the costs of doing so added to the owner’s account.

3. Prerequisites

  • All owners are required to produce a current vaccination certificate prior to acceptance into the cattery.
  • Owners are expected to provide all necessary dietary, behavioural and medical information pertaining to their cats. The cattery is not responsible for accommodating special needs without notice.
  • Cats should be in good health and any health or behavioural issues should be communicated prior to admittance to the cattery. The cattery owners have the discretion to refuse to accept any cat deemed unsuitable.
  • The cattery owners have the right to refuse to accept any cat which appears ill or excessively aggressive on arrival.
  • We do not accept un-neutered male cats (some exceptions may be allowed for cats under 6 months)

4. General

  • Cats must be caged for pick up and drop off. Please do not remove cats from cages outside of secure areas of cattery.
  • Any cat showing indications of carrying parasites (worms or fleas) may be treated at the owner’s cost so as not to impact the comfort and heath of other cats in residence.
  • The vet will be called at the owner’s cost if considered necessary, for example if the cat should become ill or be injured.
  • Cats will be deemed abandoned if they have not been picked up within 14 days of the agreed check-out date, with no contact from owner or other appropriate party. In such cases, alternative arrangements for the care of the cat will be made.
  • The cattery owners have agreed to take all reasonable measures for the care, security and safety of your pet. However, the cattery accepts no legal responsibility for your cat’s illness, loss, injury or death.
  • Shared accomodation - only cats from the same home will be allowed to board together in a shared run and shared run arrangements will require special permission from the cat owner given the risk of possible injury due to cat flights and aggressive play.

5. Cancellation

  • Sometimes plans change & disasters happen - for whatever reason, please advise us as soon as possible if you wish to cancel your cat’s booking to stay with us.
  • As a general rule cancellation 48hrs or more before check in date does not incur any fee. Cancellation later than this, but before the day of expected check-in will incur an administration fee equivalent to 1 day stay. Cancellation after the expected check-in will incur full fees.
  • During special high demand holiday periods such as Christmas - New Year, Easter and Kings Birthday weekend, we prefer a minimum of 3 days notice of cancellations prior to check in date to avoid fees.
  • Fees may be waived in exceptional circumstances subject to the discretion of cattery management.
